#### **Card 4: Inertia**

**NEW**: "The tendency of an object to resist changes in its state of motion. Objects with greater

**NEW**: "The net force is the vector sum of all forces acting on an object. When the net force is

forces are balanced, there's no acceleration. Example: a book resting on a table has balanced
forces - gravity pulling down and the table pushing up."
